Barcelona

Your trip to Barcelona will be full of culture, color, and the beauty of Catalonia. Where should you begin your explorations? Eye guzzle if you will the spires of La Sagrada Familia Church tickling the sky. The charge was designed by architect Antoni Gaudi and will leave you breathless. Afterward, take a tour of another Gaudi dream-turned-architectural reality in the Palau Guell. Admiring stonework can build a hearty appetite, so go for afternoon tapas in Placa Reial, a square in the Gothic Quarter. If you want to really feel the pulse of the city, watch a soccer (football, in these parts) game at Camp Nou. The frenetic energy of the stadium is palpable, and you’ll feel like a local in no time.

Moscow

Enjoy our great offers on flights to Moscow (MOW), Russia. The Russian capital is a vast and varied city with lots to keep visitors occupied. A visit to Red Square with the Kremlin and the famous onion domes of St. Basil's is a must for every visitor. There's also an endless list of cultural and architectural attractions to suit every taste, including the Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts, the Cosmonaut Museum and the impressive 'Seven Sisters' - seven Soviet skyscrapers that dominate the city skyline.
